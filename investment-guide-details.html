Investment Guide Details

Why Invest in Zambia?

  • Strategic location in Southern Africa
  • Abundant natural resources
  • Stable political environment
  • Growing economy and market
  • Young and trainable workforce
  • Investment incentives and protections

Investment Procedures

  1. Company Registration: Register with the Patents and Companies Registration Agency (PACRA)
  2. Tax Registration: Obtain a Tax Identification Number from the Zambia Revenue Authority
  3. Investment License: Apply for an investment license from the Zambia Development Agency (ZDA)
  4. Sector-specific Licenses: Obtain any necessary sector-specific licenses or permits

Investment Incentives

  • Tax holidays for qualifying investments
  • Duty-free importation of machinery and equipment
  • Accelerated depreciation on capital equipment
  • Guarantees and protections under the ZDA Act

Key Sectors for Investment

  • Mining and mineral processing
  • Agriculture and agro-processing
  • Energy and renewable resources
  • Manufacturing
  • Tourism and hospitality
  • Information and Communication Technology

Legal Framework

Zambia has a robust legal framework to protect investments, including:

  • The Investment Promotion and Protection Act
  • The Companies Act
  • The Zambia Development Agency Act
  • Various bilateral investment treaties
