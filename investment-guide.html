Why Invest in Zambia?

Strategic Location

Heart of Southern Africa

Natural Resources

Abundant minerals and fertile land

Stable Environment

Pro-business policies

Growing Workforce

Young and educated population

Investment Process

  1. Research potential sectors and opportunities
  2. Contact the Zambia Development Agency for guidance
  3. Register your business with PACRA (Patents and Companies Registration Agency)
  4. Obtain necessary licenses and permits
  5. Set up operations and start your business

Investment Incentives

  • Tax holidays for certain sectors and regions
  • Duty-free importation of capital equipment
  • Repatriation of profits and dividends
  • Protection against nationalization
