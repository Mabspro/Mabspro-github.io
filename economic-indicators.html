Economic Outlook

Zambia's economy is projected to continue its recovery path, supported by improved performance in the mining, agriculture, and tourism sectors. The government's commitment to economic reforms and debt restructuring is expected to boost investor confidence and contribute to sustainable growth.

Key factors influencing the economic outlook include:

  • Global commodity prices, particularly copper
  • Implementation of structural reforms
  • Climate conditions affecting agricultural output
  • Regional and global economic trends
